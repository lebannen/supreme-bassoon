# French A1 Course - Alex's Lyon Journey

## Complete Course Generation Plan

**Status:** Ready for Review
**Date:** January 2025
**Approach:** Direct Method (French-only content)
**Protagonist:** Alex (male, American newcomer to Lyon)

---

## Overview

### Two-Course Structure

**Course 1: French A1.1 - "L'Arrivée" (The Arrival)**

- **Slug:** `french-a1-1-alex-lyon`
- **Modules:** 1-10
- **Episodes:** 30 (3 per module)
- **Estimated Hours:** 20 hours
- **Focus:** Basic interactions, identity, immediate environment, survival vocabulary
- **Setting:** Lyon, France - Airport → Apartment → Neighborhood → Market

**Course 2: French A1.2 - "La Vie Quotidienne" (Daily Life)**

- **Slug:** `french-a1-2-alex-lyon`
- **Modules:** 11-20
- **Episodes:** 30 (3 per module)
- **Estimated Hours:** 20 hours
- **Focus:** Routines, complex transactions, past events, future plans
- **Setting:** Lyon - Home → Work → Social life → Travel

---

## Key Principles

### 1. French-Only Content (Direct Method)

- **NO English in episode content** (stories, dialogues)
- Start with ultra-simple, cognate-rich language
- Use context, repetition, and visual situations to convey meaning
- Progress very gradually in complexity
- Exercise instructions remain in English (UI/UX)

### 2. Immersive Storytelling

- Follow Alex's authentic journey as a newcomer
- Real-life situations with natural consequences
- Characters reappear throughout (continuity)
- Lyon-specific cultural references

### 3. Grammar Integration

- Grammar taught implicitly through patterns
- Repetition of structures before variation
- No explicit grammar rules in French content (embed in exercises)

## A1.1 - "L'Arrivée" - Detailed Module Breakdown

### Module 1: L'Aéroport (The Airport - Bienvenue)

**Theme:** First contact with French
**Grammar:** Subject Pronouns (Je, Tu, Vous), Verb Être (singular)
**Estimated Time:** 120 minutes

#### Episode 1: Le Contrôle des Passeports (Passport Control)

- **Type:** DIALOGUE
- **Characters:** Alex, Agent de Contrôle
- **Generate Audio:** Yes
- **Content Focus:**
    - Bonjour, bonsoir (greetings)
    - Passeport, s'il vous plaît
    - Merci, au revoir
- **Vocabulary:** bonjour, bonsoir, passeport, merci, s'il vous plaît, au revoir
- **French-Only Approach:** Ultra-simple, formulaic greetings and requests
- **Exercise Count:** 8-9 exercises

    - 2× Fill in Blank (bonjour/bonsoir usage)

    - 1× Sentence Scramble (Passeport, s'il vous plaît)

## French-Only Content Progression Strategy

### Modules 1-3: Ultra-Simple French

**Approach:**

- Use only formulaic, high-frequency phrases
- Repetition is key (same phrase multiple times)
- Context is 100% clear from situation
- Cognates when possible (parking, taxi, etc.)
- Physical actions described in French

**Example (Module 1, Episode 1):**

```
Agent: Bonjour.
```

- Longer exchanges
- Complex sentences emerge
- Less repetition needed

### Modules 18-20: Fluent A1

**Approach:**

- Natural French conversation

- Complex thoughts expressed simply
- Authentic A1-level French

## Exercise Distribution Strategy

### Per Episode: 8-9 Exercises (minimum)

**Typical Distribution:**

1. **Multiple Choice** (2-3 exercises)
    - Comprehension questions
    - Vocabulary recognition
    - Grammar concept checks

2. **Fill in the Blank** (2 exercises)
    - Single blank for focused grammar
    - Key vocabulary production

3. **Matching** (1 exercise)
    - Vocabulary pairs
    - Phrases to contexts
    - Grammar patterns

4. **Sentence Scramble** (1 exercise)
    - Word order practice
    - Sentence structure

5. **Listening** (1 exercise)
    - Audio comprehension
    - Pronunciation practice

6. **Cloze Reading** (1 exercise)
    - Integrated comprehension
    - Multiple blanks, context-based

### Review Episodes (M10E3, M20E3): 9-12 Exercises

- More comprehensive
- All exercise types represented
- Higher difficulty
- Integrated skills
