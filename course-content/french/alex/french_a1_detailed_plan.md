# Complete French A1 Course Plan (A1.1 & A1.2)

**Total Modules:** 20 (10 for A1.1, 10 for A1.2)
**Method:** Immersive Storyline (Direct Method compatible)
**Protagonist:** Alex, a newcomer to Lyon.

---

# PART 1: French A1.1 - "L'Arrivée" (The Arrival)

**Focus:** Basic interactions, identity, immediate environment, and survival vocabulary.

### Module 1: The Airport (Bienvenue)

* **Story:** Alex arrives at Lyon-Saint Exupéry Airport and must navigate passport control and find a taxi.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Passport Control.** (Greetings: *Bonjour, Bonsoir*, Presenting ID).
    2. **Lesson 2: The Taxi Driver.** (Asking "How are you?", Politeness: *S'il vous plaît/Merci*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Signs & Symbols.** (Alphabet, Reading signs: *Sortie, Entrée, Toilettes*).
* **Grammar:** Subject Pronouns (*Je, Tu, Vous*), Verb *Être* (Singular forms).

### Module 2: The Apartment Hunt (Qui est-ce ?)

* **Story:** Alex meets the landlord, Monsieur Dupont, to view a studio apartment.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Who are you?** (Introductions, Nationality, Professions).
    2. **Lesson 2: The Key.** (Numbers 0-10, Basic objects: *Clé, Porte*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Yes or No?** (Basic questions, Affirmation/Negation).
* **Grammar:** Verb *Être* (Plural), Nationalities (Adjectives), Negation (*Ne... pas*).

### Module 3: Moving In (La Maison)

* **Story:** The apartment is empty. Alex visits IKEA/flea market to get basics.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Box.** (Nouns: *Table, Chaise, Lit*. Gender: *Un/Une*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Where is the cat?** (Prepositions: *Dans, Sur, Sous*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Colors of the wall.** (Adjective agreement basics, Colors).
* **Grammar:** Indefinite Articles (*Un, Une, Des*), *Il y a* (There is).

### Module 4: The Neighbors (Les Autres)

* **Story:** A neighbor knocks on the door. Alex describes them to a friend later.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Knock Knock.** (Verb *Avoir*: Age, "I have a package").
    2. **Lesson 2: Describe Him.** (Physical description: *Grand, Petit, Blond*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Numbers 11-69.** (Exchanging phone numbers).
* **Grammar:** Verb *Avoir* (Present), Adjective placement (BAGS - Beauty, Age, Goodness, Size).

### Module 5: The Bakery (La Nourriture 1)

* **Story:** Alex is hungry and goes to the "Boulangerie" downstairs.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Baguette.** (Cultural focus: Greetings in shops, "Je voudrais").
    2. **Lesson 2: Pastries.** (Definite Articles: *Le, La, Les* - Specific preferences).
    3. **Lesson 3: Paying.** (Prices, "Combien ça coûte ?").
* **Grammar:** *C'est* vs. *Ce sont*, Definite vs. Indefinite Articles review.

### Module 6: Calling Home (La Famille)

* **Story:** Alex video calls family back home to give an update.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Family Tree.** (Family members vocabulary).
    2. **Lesson 2: My Brother, Your Sister.** (Possessive Adjectives: *Mon, Ma, Mes*).
    3. **Lesson 3: They live in...** (Verbs *Habiter*, *Parler* - Regular -ER verbs introduction).
* **Grammar:** Possessive Adjectives, -ER Verbs conjugation (Singular).

### Module 7: The Calendar (Le Temps)

* **Story:** Alex needs to set up an internet appointment and sign up for a gym.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Week.** (Days of the week, "Aujourd'hui, Demain").
    2. **Lesson 2: The Appointment.** (Months, Dates, "J'ai rendez-vous").
    3. **Lesson 3: What time?** (Telling time, *Heure*, *Minutes*).
* **Grammar:** Question words (*Quand ? À quelle heure ?*), -ER Verbs (Plural).

### Module 8: Lost in the City (La Ville)

* **Story:** Alex tries to find the City Hall but gets lost in "Vieux Lyon".
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Map.** (Places: *Poste, Banque, Musée*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Turn Left.** (Imperative Mode: *Tournez, Allez, Continuez*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Transport.** (Verb *Aller*, Prepositions *Au, À la*).
* **Grammar:** Verb *Aller* (Irregular), Prepositions with places (*au, à la, à l', aux*).

### Module 9: The Market (Les Quantités)

* **Story:** Sunday morning at the "Marché Saint-Antoine". Buying fresh food.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Fruits & Veggies.** (Vocabulary expansion).
    2. **Lesson 2: A Kilo of...** (Quantities: *Un kilo de, Beaucoup de, Un peu de*).
    3. **Lesson 3: I like/I hate.** (Preference verbs: *Aimer, Adorer, Détester*).
* **Grammar:** The Partitive Article (*Du, De la*), *Pas de* (Negative quantity).

### Module 10: The Apéro (Review & Social)

* **Story:** Alex hosts a small "Apéro" (drinks/snacks) for the new neighbors.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Cheers!** (Drinks, Toasting, Cultural norms of Apéro).
    2. **Lesson 2: Conversation.** (Review of introductions, hobbies basics).
    3. **Lesson 3: A1.1 Checkpoint.** (Integrated Story Quiz).
* **Grammar:** Review of Être/Avoir/Aller/-ER verbs.

# PART 2: French A1.2 - "La Vie Quotidienne" (Daily Life)

**Focus:** Routines, complex transactions, past events, and future plans.

### Module 11: Morning Routine (Le Quotidien)

* **Story:** Alex's alarm clock rings. The daily grind begins.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Waking Up.** (Reflexive Verbs: *Se réveiller, Se lever*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Bathroom Habits.** (Toiletries, *Se laver, Se brosser*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Getting Dressed.** (Clothing vocabulary basics).
* **Grammar:** Pronominal Verbs (Present tense).

### Module 12: The Commute (Le Transport)

* **Story:** The Metro is broken. Alex has to take the bus or walk.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: How do you go?** (Prepositions *En* vs *À*: *En bus, À pied*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Buying Tickets.** (Interactions at the counter/machine).
    3. **Lesson 3: Late!** (Apologizing: *Je suis en retard, Désolé*).
* **Grammar:** Verb *Prendre* (Irregular), *Descendre*.

### Module 13: At the Office (Le Travail)

* **Story:** Alex starts the job. Meeting colleagues and understanding tasks.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Office Supplies.** (*Ordi, Stylo, Bureau*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Formal Email.** (Reading simple emails, *Monsieur/Madame*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Can I...?** (Verb *Pouvoir* - Permission/Ability).
* **Grammar:** Modal Verbs (*Pouvoir, Vouloir, Devoir*).

### Module 14: Lunch Break (Le Restaurant)

* **Story:** Colleagues take Alex to a "Bouchon Lyonnais" (traditional restaurant).
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: The Menu.** (Starters, Mains, Desserts).
    2. **Lesson 2: Ordering.** (*Je vais prendre...* vs *Je voudrais*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Problems.** (Asking for water, "It's cold", The bill).
* **Grammar:** *Boire* (Irregular), Question inversion (*Voulez-vous?*).

### Module 15: Leisure & Hobbies (Les Loisirs)

* **Story:** It's the weekend. Alex looks for a sports club or music class.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Sports.** (Verb *Faire* + *du/de la*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Games/Music.** (Verb *Jouer* + *au/aux* vs *du/de la*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Frequency.** (*Souvent, Parfois, Jamais, Tous les jours*).
* **Grammar:** Verb *Faire* (Irregular), Preposition contraction (*de+le=du, à+le=au*).

### Module 16: The Shopping Spree (La Mode)

* **Story:** Alex needs a warm coat for winter.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Clothing & Materials.** (*Coton, Laine, Cuir*).
    2. **Lesson 2: The Fitting Room.** (Size, "It fits well", "It's too big").
    3. **Lesson 3: Comparisons.** (This one is *more expensive than* that one).
* **Grammar:** Demonstrative Adjectives (*Ce, Cet, Cette, Ces*), Comparatives (*Plus... que*).

### Module 17: Not Feeling Well (La Santé)

* **Story:** Alex catches a cold and visits a pharmacy/doctor.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Body Parts.** (Head, Throat, Stomach).
    2. **Lesson 2: I have pain.** (*J'ai mal à...*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Pharmacy.** (Medicine, Advice: *Il faut*).
* **Grammar:** *Il faut* (Necessity), *Au / À la* with body parts.

### Module 18: Planning the Holiday (Le Futur)

* **Story:** Summer is coming. Alex plans a trip to the South of France.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Weather.** (*Il fait beau, Il y a du soleil*).
    2. **Lesson 2: Booking.** (Hotel vs. Camping, Dates).
    3. **Lesson 3: Projects.** ("I am going to swim", "We are going to visit").
* **Grammar:** **Le Futur Proche** (*Aller* + Infinitive).

### Module 19: The Postcard (Le Passé 1)

* **Story:** Alex returns from the trip and tells friends about it.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: What happened?** (Introduction to Past Tense concept).
    2. **Lesson 2: Regular Verbs.** (*J'ai mangé, J'ai visité*).
    3. **Lesson 3: Irregular Verbs.** (*J'ai fait, J'ai eu, J'ai été*).
* **Grammar:** **Le Passé Composé** with *Avoir* (Basics only).

### Module 20: Looking Back, Looking Forward (Bilan)

* **Story:** Alex celebrates one year in France.
* **Lessons:**
    1. **Lesson 1: Memories.** (More past tense practice).
    2. **Lesson 2: Emotions.** (Expressing feelings about the year).
    3. **Lesson 3: Final A1 Exam.** (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing).
* **Grammar:** Review of Future vs. Past vs. Present.
