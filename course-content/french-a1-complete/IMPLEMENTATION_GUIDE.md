## Module 6: Sophie's Daily Routine

### Structure
**Episodes:** 2
**Theme:** Daily Life and Time

### Episode 1: Un Jour Typique (A Typical Day)
**Type:** STORY
**Sophie's narration of her daily routine**

**Story outline:**
- Sophie wakes up at 7:00 AM (Je me réveille à sept heures)
- She gets ready for work (shower, breakfast, getting dressed)
- Commutes to work
- Works from 9 AM to 6 PM
- Evening activities (dinner, reading, relaxing)
- Goes to bed around 11 PM

**Grammar focus:** Reflexive verbs (se réveiller, se laver, s'habiller, se coucher)
**Vocabulary:** Time, daily activities, reflexive verbs, frequency adverbs

**Exercise types:**
1. Multiple choice: What time does Sophie wake up?
2. Fill in blank: Je _____ à sept heures (me réveille)
3. Matching: Daily activities French-English
4. Sentence scramble: Reflexive verb sentences
5. Cloze reading: Sophie's routine
6. Listening: Time expressions
7. Multiple choice: Comprehension questions
8. Fill in blank: Time expressions

### Episode 2: Le Weekend vs La Semaine
**Type:** DIALOGUE
**Sophie and Thomas compare their routines**

**Dialogue outline:**
Thomas asks Sophie about her weekend routine vs. weekday
- Weekday: strict schedule, early wake-up
- Weekend: sleeping in, relaxed activities, hobbies
- Discussion of what they do on Saturdays vs. Sundays

**Characters:** Sophie (Puck), Thomas (Achird)
**Grammar focus:** Present tense, frequency adverbs, time expressions

## Exercise Template for Each Episode

For **EACH episode**, include at least 8 exercises:

1. **Multiple Choice** - Comprehension question
2. **Multiple Choice** - Vocabulary or detail question
3. **Matching** - Vocabulary pairs (5 pairs)
4. **Fill in the Blank** - Grammar or vocabulary
5. **Sentence Scramble** - Word order practice
6. **Fill in the Blank** - Multi-blank or different grammar point
7. **Cloze Reading** - Summary with multiple blanks
8. **Multiple Choice or Listening** - Additional comprehension

### Exercise Guidelines:
- `estimatedDurationSeconds`: 30-60 for multiple choice, 90 for matching, 120-180 for cloze reading
- `pointsValue`: 10-15 for simple exercises, 20-25 for complex ones
- `difficultyRating`: 1.0-2.0 for A1 level
- `isRequired`: true for most, false for bonus exercises
